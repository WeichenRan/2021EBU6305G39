    About Our Museum

    Exhibition of Representative Artifacts of Chinese History

    Welcome to the Chinese History Network Museum, where we showcase artifacts representative of 5,000 years of Chinese history from its origins to the modern era.


    The museum divides Chinese history into six sections: Chinese Origins, Spring and Autumn Period, Warring States Period, Qin and Han Period, Tang and Song Period, Ming and Qing Period, and Republican Period. These periods have contributed to the development of China's long-standing civilization, and all of them have contributed to and represented the development of Chinese civilization. We will display artifacts representative of these different eras.


    Our exhibits show the development of Chinese civilization over thousands of years, and by viewing the artifacts, you will gain a better understanding of the lifestyle and cultural practices of the Chinese people during the ancient period. In addition, the museum also offers a display of creative products related to the exhibition, so that you can feel the charm of traditional Chinese culture more personally!
