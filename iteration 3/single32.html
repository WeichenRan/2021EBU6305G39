    Liberation Memorial Cup

    • Dynasty: Modern
    • Category: daily necessities
    • Museum: National Museum of China
    • Size: height 6cm, caliber 5cm

    It has a long belly and a circular handle on the belly. Colorful patterns on the outer wall. There is a blue and white string pattern around the mouth of the cup, and a five-color flag and an 18 star flag are painted on the abdomen. The two flags cross each other. There is an ink book on it: "restoration Memorial", and next to the flag is an ink book: "souvenir of the Republic of China". The five color flag is the flag used when the provisional government of the Republic of China was established. The five colors of red, yellow, blue, white and black represent the Republic of the five ethnic groups of Han, Manchu, Mongolian, Hui and Tibet. The 18 star flag is the flag of the Hubei military government. The 18 stars represent the 18 provinces at that time. The ware has regular production, bright glaze and bright colors. It is a standard instrument for identifying the porcelain of the Republic of China and has important historical value.
