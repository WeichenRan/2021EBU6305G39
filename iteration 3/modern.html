    Republic of China

    The Republic of China (1912-1949) was located in eastern Asia and east of the Pacific Ocean. It was the national name and year number of China during the establishment of the Qing Dynasty to the establishment of the People's Republic of China.The Republic of China is the first Democratic Republic of Asia established after the 1911 Revolution, referred to as the Republic of China.
