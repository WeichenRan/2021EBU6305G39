    Tang and Song Periods

    During the Qin and Han Periods, the two unified dynasties of the Qin Dynasty and the Han Dynasty in Chinese history. Qin and Han dynasties were a period of transition in Chinese society. The period of Chinese cultural integration was also the first period of strong in Chinese history.The national governance system formed during this period not only profoundly affects China's historical process, has formed the basics of Han, Chinese, Chinese characters, and Hanfu, but also determines the basic pattern of Chinese culture in the future.
